Books

The Templars: The Rise and Spectacular Fall of God's Holy Warriors, gives a detailed account of entirety of the history of the Knigts Templar, demystifying common misconceptions along the way (ISBN: 978-0525428305).

Knights Templar Encyclopedia: The Essential Guide to the People, Places, Events, and Symbols of the Order of the Temple, provides an alphabetized view of important terms and people in the Knights Templar (ISBN: 978-1564149268).

The Everyday Life of the Templars: The Knights Templar at Home provides readers with a glimpse of what life as a Templar was like (ISBN: 978-1781553732).

The Trial of the Templars describes the downfall of the Knights Templar starting with arrests in France in 1307 up until the order officially collapsed (ISBN: 978-0521457279).
